FAQ

Does 2key become the Merchant of Record?+

No. The seller is always the Merchant of Record.

Who appears on the customer's statement?+

The seller's legal name (Stripe/PayPal standard behavior).

Can influencers earn from subscriptions?+

Yes — including renewals, if the seller enables it.

Can sellers bring their own Stripe account?+

Yes. Required.

Does 2key touch customer funds?+

Only as a platform commission, routed via Stripe/PayPal.
